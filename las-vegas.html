Welcome To Las Vegas

This is the official page of the "Sin City", Las Vegas by the Nevada Government Office. This webpage is dedicated to helping those in need to navigate this densely populated and always awake city.

History of Las Vegas

Las Vegas was founded in 1905 and mainly served as a resting place for travelers on the way to California. The city gained traction not long after Reno with the development of its famous Vegas Strip and the legalization of gambling.

With its rich and infamous history, Las Vegas has become a global hub well known for entertainment and tourism with a bright nightlife, luxurious resorts, shopping centers, and world-class entertainment.

Fun Facts About Las Vegas

  • The city population is 653,000 as of 2023 (United States Census Bureau)
  • Las Vegas was officially incorporated into Nevada in 1911 (City of Las Vegas)
  • Las Vegas is apart of Clark county on the southern region of the state
  • Las Vegas is the most populated city in the entire state of Nevada, making it a densely populated urban city
  • Las Vegas has a median household income of $68,905 compared to the state median of $72,333 (United States Census Bureau)
